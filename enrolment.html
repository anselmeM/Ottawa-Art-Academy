Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our virtual classroom and curriculum.

What materials will I need for my first class? +
For our drawing curriculum: graphite pencils (2H, HB, 2B), nitram vine charcoal, a drawing board, and kneaded erasers. For our oil painting courses: canvas panels, odorless mineral spirits, linseed oil, and our base paint pigments (Titanium White, Ivory Black, Raw Umber, and primaries). A full material checklist is emailed immediately upon registration.
Can I enroll as an absolute beginner? +
Yes, absolute beginners are welcome. Our academic curriculum is step-by-step. All students begin by mapping plaster casts (bargue sheets) and geometric volumes, which builds visual vocabulary, shape accuracy, and value control before moving to color and paint handling.
How are critiques delivered in virtual sessions? +
Students upload clear photographs of their drawings or paintings to our online review board before each class. During the live stream, the instructor overlays digital annotations directly onto the student's work to show line and value corrections, explaining adjustments in real-time.
What is the cancellation or refund policy? +
Monthly tuition plans can be cancelled or paused at any time with a 7-day notice prior to the next billing date. Specific weekend intensive workshops are refundable up to 14 days before the workshop start date.
