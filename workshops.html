Virtual Demos & Workshops

Currently, all academy classes, workshops, and demos are held live online. Connect with our studio and receive direct, real-time critiques from our instructors.

Figure Drawing & Anatomy

TUESDAYS, 6-9 PM | ONLINE LIVE
Learn to draw the human figure dynamically with structural accuracy. This course teaches gesture, planar breakdown, structural anatomy, and values using charcoal.
Critiques are delivered live using screen annotation tools. Open to beginner and intermediate artists.
Enrol - $240/Mo

Classical Cast Drawing

THURSDAYS, 6-9 PM | ONLINE LIVE
Develop accurate observation skills through classical cast studies. In this course, we explore shape mapping, shadow lines, value organization, and rendering textures in graphite.
Crucial for building a solid foundation in drawing before starting painting. Includes weekly video review assignments.
Enrol - $240/Mo

Academic Oil Painting

SATURDAYS, 10 AM - 1 PM | ONLINE LIVE
A comprehensive course in classical oil painting. Focus on still life and portraits while learning canvas preparation, brush control, pigment attributes, and the Munsell color system (value, hue, chroma).
Instruction includes detailed underpainting reviews and glaze techniques. Some painting experience is recommended.
Enrol - $240/Mo

How Our Live Studio Streams Work

BROADCAST IN 4K QUALITY | MULTI-CAM FEED
Traditional art study relies heavily on visual demonstration. Our virtual courses are broadcast from a specialized studio equipped with dual-camera angles:
1. **The Board/Easel View**: Close-up, shadow-accurate camera showing the exact pencil strokes, paint applications, and edges on the drawing board.
2. **The Palette View**: Shows color mixing ratios, medium usage, and pigment mixtures in real-time.
All feeds are accessible live, with audio channels open for immediate student inquiries.

Special Guest Seminars

MONTHLY WEEKEND WORKSHOPS | GUEST MASTERS
Throughout the year, we host specialized weekend intensives led by internationally recognized painters and draftsmen. These sessions cover portrait drawing, plein air methods, anatomy lectures, and sculptural modeling.
Enrolled Academy students receive priority booking and a 20% discount on guest seminars. Register early as participant spots are capped to ensure high-quality critique time.
